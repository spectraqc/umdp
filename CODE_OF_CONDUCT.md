# Code of Conduct

UMDP follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of role, employer, experience level, or background.

## Expected behaviour

- Show empathy and kindness toward other participants.
- Disagree constructively. Critique ideas, not people.
- Accept that broadcasters, studios, and vendors have differing real-world constraints. UMDP exists to encode those, not to litigate them.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks.
- Sharing confidential or NDA-protected delivery specs.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or vendor lock-in arguments dressed as schema proposals.

## Reporting

To report an incident, open an issue tagged `coc` or, for sensitive matters, email the maintainers (see `MAINTAINERS.md`). All reports are reviewed in confidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or revoke contributor access in response to behaviour they consider inappropriate. Persistent or severe violations may result in a permanent ban from the project.
